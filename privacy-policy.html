Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 27, 2026

Zyniq is a social finance application that helps users manage shared expenses, personal finance records, balances, commitments, reminders, loans, savings products, and related financial activity. This Privacy Policy explains how Zyniq collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the mobile application.

📋 Information We Collect

Zyniq may collect the following types of information:

Account Information

When you sign in, we may collect account information provided through Firebase Authentication, such as:

  • Email address
  • Display name, if available
  • Firebase user identifier
  • Authentication tokens required to securely identify your account

Financial And App Data

To provide the app's features, Zyniq may collect and store information that you enter into the app, including:

  • Expenses
  • Groups and group members
  • Balances and settlements
  • Budgets and loans
  • Investment plans and contributions
  • Savings or chitty fund records
  • Reminders
  • Activity related to shared finance features

Usage, Diagnostics, And Performance Data

Zyniq uses Firebase services that may collect technical and diagnostic information, including:

  • App usage events and crash reports
  • Performance metrics
  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version
  • Approximate diagnostic identifiers
  • Error logs

These services help us understand app stability, improve performance, and fix issues.

Locally Stored Data

The app may store certain information on your device using secure storage or local preferences, such as authentication state, app settings, or cached app data.

🔍 How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide account sign-in and authentication
  • Sync your account with the Zyniq backend
  • Show your personal and shared finance information
  • Manage groups, expenses, balances, commitments, and reminders
  • Improve app reliability and performance
  • Detect and fix crashes or technical problems
  • Maintain security and prevent unauthorized access
  • Provide customer support if requested

🔗 Third-Party Services

Zyniq uses third-party services, including Firebase services provided by Google. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

Firebase services used by Zyniq may include:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics
  • Firebase Performance Monitoring
  • Firebase Remote Config

Google Firebase Privacy and Security information: firebase.google.com/support/privacy

Google Privacy Policy: policies.google.com/privacy
